About

I am a doctoral student at the National Engineering Research Center for Electric Vehicles, BIT, under the supervision of Prof. Wenwei Wang.

My research focuses on battery state estimation and smart battery technologies. I am dedicated to developing intelligent systems that enhance our understanding of battery behavior and enable advanced energy solutions beyond conventional capabilities.
